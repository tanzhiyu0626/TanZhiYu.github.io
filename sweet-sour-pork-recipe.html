Ingredients

For Pork

  • 400g pork belly or shoulder, cut into 2cm cubes
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

For Vegetables

  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 small onion, cut into chunks
  • 8-10 pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

For Sauce

  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1/5

Season pork with salt and pepper. In a bowl, beat egg, then add pork and toss to coat. Mix cornstarch and flour, then coat pork pieces evenly.

Step 2/5

Heat oil to 170°C (340°F) in a deep pan or wok. Fry pork in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step 3/5

For the sauce: Mix sugar, vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, and water in a bowl. Set aside.

Step 4/5

Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok. Sauté garlic until fragrant, then add bell peppers and onion. Stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add pineapple and cook 1 minute more.

Step 5/5

Pour sauce into the wok and bring to a simmer. Add cornstarch mixture and stir until thickened. Add fried pork, toss to coat evenly. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve hot with rice.
