Ingredients

10 Servings

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 10 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 200g fresh salmon
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • Soy sauce (for serving)

Preparation

Step 1/6

Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear, then cook according to package.

Step 2/6

Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into cooked rice.

Step 3/6

Place nori on bamboo mat, spread rice evenly.

Step 4/6

Add salmon and cucumber slices.

Step 5/6

Roll tightly, then slice into 8 pieces.

Step 6/6

Serve with soy sauce.
