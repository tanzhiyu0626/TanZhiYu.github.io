Ingredients

9 Servings

  • Fresh rice noodle sheets (bánh phở cuốn)
  • 200g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh herbs: mint, basil, cilantro
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Cucumber & carrots (julienned)
  • Dipping Sauce (Nước Chấm): fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, garlic, chili

Preparation

Step 1/4

Marinate beef with garlic, soy/fish sauce, and oil. Stir-fry quickly.

Step 2/4

Wash herbs, prepare lettuce, slice cucumber & carrot.

Step 3/4

Lay a rice noodle sheet, add lettuce, herbs, beef & veggies, then roll tightly.

Step 4/4

Cut into bite-sized rolls and serve with dipping sauce.
