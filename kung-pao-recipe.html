Ingredients

For Main Dish

  • 300g chicken breast, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 100g peanuts (roasted, unsalted)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3-4 dried red chilies, cut into sections (adjust to taste)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 spring onions, cut into 3cm sections
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

For Marinade

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

For Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp chicken stock (or water)
  • 1 tsp cornstarch, mixed with 1 tbsp water

Preparation

Step 1/5

Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine chicken cubes with egg white, marinade ingredients, and 1 tbsp cornstarch. Mix well and set aside for 15 minutes.

Step 2/5

Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, mix all sauce ingredients except the cornstarch mixture. Set aside.

Step 3/5

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add peanuts and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Remove and set aside.

Step 4/5

In the same wok, add remaining oil. Add dried chilies, garlic, and ginger; stir-fry for 30 seconds until aromatic. Add chicken and stir-fry until almost cooked through (about 3-4 minutes).

Step 5/5

Add bell peppers and spring onions, stir-fry for 1 minute. Pour in the sauce, cook until it starts to thicken. Add peanuts and the cornstarch mixture, stir quickly to coat evenly. Serve hot with rice.
