Ingredients (4 servings)

14 Servings

  • 1.5–2 kg beef bones (or chicken bones for phở gà)
  • 400–500 g beef brisket or thinly sliced sirloin (or shredded chicken)
  • 500 g dried rice noodles (bánh phở)
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 piece of ginger (7–8 cm), halved
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 3–4 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1–2 tbsp rock sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh garnishes: bean sprouts, Thai basil, cilantro, scallions, lime, sliced chili
  • Optional sauces: hoisin & sriracha

Preparation

Step 1/8

Parboil bones: Boil bones for 5–10 minutes, drain and rinse.

Step 2/8

Char aromatics: Char onion and ginger until blackened, then rinse.

Step 3/8

Toast spices: Toast star anise, cinnamon, cloves, and coriander seeds until fragrant.

Step 4/8

Simmer broth: Add bones, onion, ginger, and spices to 3–3.5 L water. Simmer 3–4 hours, skimming foam.

Step 5/8

Cook brisket: Add brisket for 60–90 minutes until tender; slice thinly.

Step 6/8

Season: Strain broth, add fish sauce, rock sugar, and salt to taste.

Step 7/8

Noodles: Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.

Step 8/8

Assemble: Place noodles in bowls, add beef slices, ladle hot broth over, and garnish with herbs, sprouts, and lime.
