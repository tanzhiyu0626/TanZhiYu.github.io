Ingredients

10 skewers

  • 500g chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic (blended)
  • 2 shallots (blended)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Bamboo skewers (soaked in water)
  • Peanut sauce (for serving)

Preparation

Step 1/5

Blend garlic and shallots into paste.

Step 2/5

Mix chicken with turmeric, coriander, sugar, paste, and oil. Marinate 2–4 hours.

Step 3/5

Skewer chicken pieces onto sticks.

Step 4/5

Grill over charcoal (or oven) until cooked and slightly charred.

Step 5/5

Serve with peanut sauce, cucumber, and onion.
